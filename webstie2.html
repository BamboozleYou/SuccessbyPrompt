Problem Solving & Architecture

Debug issues, design solutions, and architect scalable systems

Solution Architecture Brainstorming

Help me brainstorm different architectural approaches for [DESCRIBE TECHNICAL CHALLENGE]. Consider scalability, performance, and maintenance requirements. Generate 3 distinct approaches with pros and cons for each.

Debugging Strategy Assistant

I'm encountering [DESCRIBE BUG/ISSUE] in my [TECHNOLOGY/FRAMEWORK] application. Walk me through a systematic debugging approach, including the most likely causes and step-by-step troubleshooting methods.

Code Review & Optimization

Review this code snippet and suggest improvements for performance, readability, and maintainability: [PASTE YOUR CODE]. Focus on best practices and potential security vulnerabilities.

Personal Skill Development

Learn new technologies and improve existing skills efficiently

Personalized Learning Path

Create a 30-day learning plan to help me master [TECHNOLOGY/SKILL] as efficiently as possible. I have [X] years of experience with [RELATED TECHNOLOGIES] and can dedicate [TIME] hours per week to learning. Include recommended resources and milestones.

Technical Skill Gap Analysis

Analyze my current technical skills as a [YOUR ROLE] with [X] years of experience in [TECHNOLOGIES]. Identify the top 3 skill gaps I should address to advance to a [TARGET ROLE] position, considering current market demands.

Learning Efficiency Optimizer

I'm learning [TECHNOLOGY] but struggling with [SPECIFIC CHALLENGE]. Suggest 5 different learning approaches tailored to my learning style: [visual/auditory/kinesthetic/reading]. Include practical exercises and ways to measure progress.

Career Enhancement

Strategic career planning and professional growth guidance

Career Transition Strategy

I want to transition from [CURRENT ROLE] to [TARGET ROLE] within [TIMEFRAME]. Create a step-by-step action plan including skills to develop, projects to build, and networking strategies. Consider my experience in [CURRENT TECHNOLOGIES].

Salary Negotiation Prep

Help me prepare for a salary negotiation for a [JOB TITLE] position. My current salary is [AMOUNT], I have [X] years of experience, and the market rate appears to be [RANGE]. Provide talking points and justifications for a [TARGET AMOUNT] request.

Professional Development Plan

Create a 12-month professional development plan for a [YOUR ROLE] looking to advance to [TARGET LEVEL]. Include specific skills, certifications, projects, and measurable goals. Consider industry trends in [YOUR FIELD].

Resume & Application Help

Optimize your resume and job applications for maximum impact

Resume Achievement Enhancement

Transform these basic job descriptions into powerful achievement statements for my resume: 1) [BASIC DESCRIPTION 1] 2) [BASIC DESCRIPTION 2] 3) [BASIC DESCRIPTION 3]. Focus on quantifiable impacts and technical expertise.

ATS-Optimized Resume Review

Review my resume for a [JOB TITLE] position at [COMPANY TYPE]. Optimize it for ATS systems by suggesting relevant keywords, improving formatting, and ensuring it aligns with this job description: [PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION].

Cover Letter Customization

Write a compelling cover letter for [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY]. Highlight how my experience in [YOUR BACKGROUND] aligns with their needs: [KEY REQUIREMENTS FROM JOB POSTING]. Make it personal and specific to this opportunity.

Interview Preparation

Ace your technical and behavioral interviews

Technical Interview Preparation

Help me prepare for a technical interview for [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY TYPE]. Generate 10 likely technical questions based on [TECHNOLOGIES/SKILLS REQUIRED] and provide detailed answers I can practice with.

STAR Method Story Builder

Help me structure this experience into a compelling STAR format story for behavioral interviews: [DESCRIBE YOUR EXPERIENCE]. Make it concise yet detailed, highlighting leadership, problem-solving, or technical skills.

Company Research & Questions

I'm interviewing at [COMPANY] for [POSITION]. Based on their business model, recent news, and industry challenges, suggest 8 thoughtful questions I can ask that demonstrate genuine interest and technical understanding.
